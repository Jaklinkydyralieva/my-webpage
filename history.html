The Beginning of Space Exploration

Space exploration began in the 20th century, with the launch of the first artificial satellite, Sputnik 1, by the Soviet Union in 1957. This marked the beginning of the Space Race, which led to numerous advancements in space technology.

Key Milestones:

  • 1961 - Yuri Gagarin becomes the first human in space.
  • 1969 - Apollo 11 lands on the Moon.
  • 1998 - The International Space Station (ISS) begins operations.
