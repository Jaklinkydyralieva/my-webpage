What is Space Exploration?


Space exploration is the investigation of outer space using advanced technologies to understand the universe, planets, stars, and the potential for life beyond Earth. It involves sending spacecraft and rovers to study distant planets, moons, and other celestial bodies, providing insights into their atmospheres, geology, and conditions for supporting life.
